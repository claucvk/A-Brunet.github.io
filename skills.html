About

I am performing both experimental and theoretical study at the single molecule level of conformational changes of the DNA molecule. I am investigate the impact of local defects included in the DNA molecule and of physicochemical conditions set by the surrounding mobiles ions presente in the solvent.

My will is to continue in the condensed matter field at the frontier with biology and physics. Besides, I am extremely eager to explore the conformations of the DNA in its native state such as it is organized in chromatin. I am very interested by the research on the genome architecture and the dynamic of chromosome folding.
